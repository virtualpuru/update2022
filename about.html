Welcome to Er. Purushottam Bhandari

Er. Purushottam Bhandari

Welcome to our 10D Villa Inc. !! We are a team of experienced professionals in Nepal who specialize in providing innovative and efficient solutions for all your building design and planning needs. Whether you are looking to construct a new building or renovate an existing one, we are here to help you every step of the way. Our company is committed to delivering high-quality services that are tailored to meet the unique requirements of each client. We believe that the key to success lies in close collaboration with our clients, which allows us to fully understand their goals, preferences, and budget constraints.

Our expertise in building design, architecture, and project management, we are able to provide a comprehensive range of services that includes feasibility studies, conceptual design, detailed engineering, construction supervision, and post-construction support. Our team is equipped with the latest tools and technologies to ensure that all projects are completed on time, within budget, and to the highest standards of quality.

At 10D Villa Inc , we are passionate about creating functional, aesthetic, and sustainable buildings that meet the needs of our clients and the communities they serve. Contact us today to learn more about how we can help you turn your vision into reality.

Yes, 2D and/or 3D drawings provided upon request as part of our design services.

Yes, you can view our Photo Galleries by selecting the tab at the top of this page
How far do you travel for jobs?
All over Nepal
Can you guide us through the design process of larger works?
Yes. We can have our architect draw up your plans and CEO, with his three of experience, can offer advice and suggestions to match your budget.
